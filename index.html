Passion. Preparation. Opportunity.


Perez Sports Associates is a full-service agency that represents professional football players. Our representation includes the following services:

Contract Negotiaion

Perez Sports, we have a thorough understanding of the NFL & CFL Collective Bargaining Agreements and salary cap systems. Our knowledge and preparation allows us to effectively negotiate fair contracts for our clients.

NFL Preparation Program

Perez Sports Associates has developed an unparalleled NFL Preparation Program. Upon signing with Perez Sports, we complete a detailed assessment of our clients’ physical strengths and weaknesses. Once their strengths and weaknesses have been identified, an individually customized workout plan is created for each client. The workout plan, which may include nutritional guidelines and a supplement program, assists our clients in improving their strength, speed, and quickness for the NFL Combine and Pro Day workouts.

Endorsements

At Perez Sports, we actively market our clients and seek endorsement opportunities as a means to generate additional income. We work tirelessly to match any endorsement opportunity with each of our clients’ individual interests, personality, and values. We negotiate all endorsement deals with the same due diligence and professionalism that we utilize when negotiating a NFL contract.

Our Team

John Perez

NFLPA/CFLPA certified contract advisor

John is a Certified Contract Advisor with both the NFLPA and CFLPA and is the Lead Contract Negotiator for Perez Sports. He has built strong professional relationships with many NFL scouts, personnel, and executives. As a result, Perez Sports is an agency with established credibility and direct access to the NFL’s decision makers. In addition, John has also served as a guest speaker on the Agent Panel at the FCS-National Bowl in Miami, Florida, an annual collegiate football all-star game.

Bryan Perez

Lead Scout

Bryan serves as Perez Sports’ Director of Player Scouting and is responsible for the evaluation of all Perez Sports’ prospective clients. Prior to becoming a Certified Contract Advisor, Bryan’s background includes having worked as a regional scout for CFL’s Ottawa REDBLACKS. Based on his experience as a scout and accuracy of his player evaluations, Bryan has gained respect from NFL scouts and management.
